Mithi Tharparkar Culture

Mithi Tharparkar, a region in Sindh, is famous for its Sufi mindset, love, peace, and hospitality. The people here have
preserved centuries-old traditions to this day,where life is based on simplicity, morality, and respect.

Music & Festivals
